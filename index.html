Travel Diary

Puri, Odisha

I visited the Jagannath Temple, one of the Char Dham sites. The energy near the Lion Gate (Singhadwara) was immense. Entering through the Lion Gate and exiting through the Elephant Gate is considered auspicious. I was fortunate to visit just after the Rath Yatra amid a calm crowd, witnessing beautiful Kalinga architecture.

After spiritual exploration, I spent time at Golden Beach, known for its wide sandy stretch and strong waves — perfect for sunset views, sand art, and camel rides. The Mahaprasad at Anand Bazaar was an unforgettable experience.

Finally, I visited the Konark Sun Temple, 35 km from Puri — a UNESCO World Heritage Site shaped like a chariot, adorned with stunning stone carvings and steeped in divine artistry.
